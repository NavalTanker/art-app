About Art App

Welcome to the Art App! This application is a versatile platform for artists and art enthusiasts.

Features

  • Showcase: Browse dynamic art galleries, view the developmental trail of each piece, and see how artworks look in different frames. Galleries are managed simply by adding images to folders on the backend.
  • Lessons (Coming Soon): A future section for tutorials and learning new art techniques.
  • Start Drawing (Coming Soon): A future interactive canvas for creating your own art directly in the app.

This app is built with Flask and containerized with Docker for easy setup and deployment.
